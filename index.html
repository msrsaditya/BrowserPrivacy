Important Note

This data does not serve as a definitive benchmark for privacy. It aggregates test results from privacytests.org across various browsers and categories.

While higher scores generally indicate better privacy protection, this primarily applies to privacy-focused browsers. Mainstream browsers may show different real-world behavior despite their scores.

Example: Opera's higher score than Safari in Desktop results from better ad-blocking, not necessarily superior overall privacy. Some browsers like Tor focus on anonymity rather than tracker blocking.

Consider these results as indicators of comprehensive privacy features rather than absolute measures. Different browsers excel in different aspects of privacy protection.
